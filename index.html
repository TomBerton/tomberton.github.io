I'm an Adjunct Professor at Austin Community College, where I teach Nutrition and Biology. I earned my B.S. from the University of Houston in Biochemistry and his PhD. from the University of Texas at Austin in Nutritional Sciences. After graduating from UT. Austin, I did Postdoctoral work at Baylor College of Medicine, in the Department of Cell Biology, and in Smithville, Texas at U.T.MD Anderson Cancer Center, Department of Molecular Carcinogensis. After my postdoctoral work I was an Instructor at U.T. MD Anderson and Texas A&M Health Science Center in the Department of Molecular Medicine. I integrated multidisciplinary fields, such as nutrition, cell and molecular biology, genetics, dermatology, histopathology,mouse models of cancer, and stem cell techniques to investigate DNA repair, apoptosis, cell cycle and hormonal regulation, and biomarkers of inflammation in the area of epidermal and mammary carcinogenesis, and metabolic syndrome.

I also help manage the Biology Department's WordPress website. I am an avid runner and compete in running events from 10K up to the marathon. I earned my Software Testing Certification at ACC in May 2016 and I am currently working getting my Data Analytics and Visualization Certification at UT Austin.
